Starter Kits

Samsung SmartThings Home Monitoring Kit

The perfect way to start building a smart home! Kit includes the the Samsung SmartThings Controller, two Samsung SmartThings Multipurpose Sensors, one Samsung SmartThings Motion Sensor, and one Samsung SmartThings Outlet. Compatible with Amazon Alexa, Zigbee and Z-Wave smart devices.

Insteon Connected Kit

The Insteon Connected Kit includes the Insteon Hub, one wireless camera, one motion sensor, one door/window sensor, one water leak sensor, and two lamp controllers. Compatible with Insteon devices, Siri, Amazon Echo, Logitech, Sonos, and Nest. Use the smarthone app to monitor your security cameras!

Insteon Starter Kit

The Insteon Starter Kit includes the Insteon Hub and two dimmer modules. This basic kit automates your lighting, but is extendable to other devices. Compatible with Insteon devices, Siri, Amazon Echo, Logitech, Sonos, and Nest.

Iris Starter Kit

The Iris Starter Kits comes in three packages: Safe and Secure, Comfort and Control, and Smart Kit. Each kit is designed to get you up and running quickly depending on the project. Professional monitoring available.

Smart Hubs

Samsung SmartThings

The Samsung SmartThings is compatible with a huge array of smart devices, and controlling them all through the smartphone app is a breeze.

Insteon Smart Hub

The Insteon Hub is compatible with Insteon devices. With Insteon, you don't need to buy range extenders to provide full coverage of your home, as each device acts as a repeater.

Wink Hub 2

The Wink Hub 2 is a gorgeous device that fits on your bookshelf. Compatible with every major smart device protocol, the Wink Hub has a large number of compatible smart devices.

Vera Control VeraLite

The Vera Lite is a cool little green box that controls over 750 devices on the Z-Wave protocol. You can set up custom notifications via text and email, and programming automation is a snap.

Devices

Ring Doorbell

Wifi enabled doorbell with camera and audio! Even when you're not home, see who's at your door. Infrared camera for evening hours!

Netgear Arlo Wire-Free Pro Security Camera

Wire-free, weatherproof, 720p HD security camera. Monitor your home from anywhere! Compatible with SmartThings and Wink.

Yale Assure Lock

Unlock your home through your smartphone! Fits in standard doors. Integrates with home automation to set up automatic door locking

Samsung SmartThings Motion Sensor

The SmartThings Motion Sensor detects movement in your home and send you alerts if you're away. Use the sensor to trigger lights or other devices when movement is detected.
